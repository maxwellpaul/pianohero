Steps on how to play Piano Hero:

1. Start up the application
2. Choose a song
3. The controls for playing the notes are a,s,d,f on the keyboard
4. As you successfully hit notes, the rock meter needle will move to the right
5. If you miss a note the rock meter needle will move to the left
6. If you miss enough notes, and the rock meter reaches all the way to the left, the game will end early
7. When the song is over, you can choose to play the song again or go back to the main menu and choose a new song
